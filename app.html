The Process

Simple, Intentional Steps

1

Browse & Choose Your Coach

Read each coach's style and vibe. Trust your gut — the right fit often announces itself. You can always switch coaches after your first session.

2

Book an Intro Session

Fill in a short form with your availability and a little about what you'd like to work on. No pressure, no wrong answers.

3

Meet in a Private Video Call

Sessions happen over a secure, private video link — 50 minutes of your time, fully focused on you. Camera optional.

4

Grow at Your Own Pace

Continue weekly, biweekly, or as-needed. There are no contracts. Your journey, your timeline — we're just here to walk it with you.
